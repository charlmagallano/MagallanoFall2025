SparkQuest

SparkQuest is a student project focused on branding and interface design for an academic management app. The goal was to create a clear visual identity and a user-friendly platform that simplifies student life. The branding uses a hand-drawn flame and spark to represent momentum and consistency, paired with clean typography for balance. The app concept centers on clarity and function, bringing together classes, finances, health, commute, and social tools into one streamlined space.
