Oishi Cracklings

Crispy, salty chips with a sharp vinegar flavor. They have a rough texture and a strong tang that makes them stand out from other snacks.

Moby Chocolate Snack

Light corn puffs coated in sweet chocolate powder. They're crunchy at first but soften as you eat them.

Oishi Potato Fries

Thin, baked potato sticks with a mild ketchup taste. They're slightly sweet and lightly seasoned.

Jack 'n Jill Roller Coaster

Round potato rings covered in orange cheese flavoring. The texture is airy and crisp, with a salty aftertaste.

Regent Snacku

Puffed rice snacks with a light vegetable seasoning. They're crunchy and not too salty, often eaten straight from the bag.

Regent Sweet Corn

Small corn puffs with a sweet corn flavor. They have a light crunch and a mild sweetness that lingers.

Possible Allergens

Milk, Soy, Wheat, Corn & Fish
