Welcome

I grew up in Pangasinan, where the sari-sari store was part of everyday life. After school, l'd grab snacks like Cracklings or Snacku to share with friends. This store brings back those familiar Filipino flavors I grew up with.

What is a sari-sari store?

A sari-sari store is a small neighborhood shop in the Philippines that sells everyday items and snacks in small quantities. It's usually run from someone's home and serves as a convenient stop for locals.

Fun Fact

My grandma on my mother's side owns a sari-sari store!
